What you just saw was the fundamental idea behind RSA, a revolutionary public-key crytography system that is widely in use today. The entire concept of RSA encryption revolves around the fact that numbers are hard to factor.

You can try this with a friend. Find two primes, and hide the private key. If they have the public key, share it to a friend, and they can encrypt a a message for you. Only you will be able to decrypt the message.
